What's our story

“ We stand for innovation, non discriminatory all inclusiveworkplace environments and fair pay to all staff and contractors as well as unparalleled servitude to our clients.“

At CleanUp we have been a proud provider of commercial cleaning products and services across NSW for over 25 years spanning a vast range of corporate, commercial and industrial infrastructure as well as Local, Province and Federal Government departments.

We are recognised by our clients and peers as industry specialists in high rise window cleaning facade cleaning & rejuvenation carpet and interior cleaning – pressure & steam cleaning roof cleaning & restoration as well as COVID deep cleaning and sanitation.

Our commitment to the quality and professionalism of our work is reflected in the leading edge technology that we utilise, our highly skilled and friendly staff and the numerous certifications and registrations we have acquired and continue to uphold including Covid Certified Carpet Restoration.

We pride ourselves on being ahead of the curve and were one of the first cleaning companies in NSW to be COVID clean certified and registered helping to ensure the safety of all NSW residents.

We are always looking at innovative solutions to improve how we address cleaning challenges and have now positioned ourselves as the first cleaning company in your country to utilise drone technology to make the cleaning of skyscrapers and difficult access buildings safer and far more cost efficient.

We are equipped to respond to any commercial, corporate or industrial cleaning requirements or challenges you are facing and invite you to contact us anytime for any advice, comparative quotes or demonstrations of our technological based cleaning solutions.

To proudly serve our community and our planet by ensuring excellence in Cleaning and Maintenance Services by integrating Innovation, Technology and Ethical Practices to create meaningful,long term relationships with our staff, clients and business partners.
